MEET THE TEAM

Arthus Dallas

Captain/Ship's Master

Born in February 27, 2075, in Wolf Point, Montana and grew up in Port Arthur, Texas. Captain Dallas obtained his spaceflight license from Mercerton Flight Academy, after which he served a three-year stint as pilot and navigator aboard the salvage vessel USCSS Snark. Following this, he transferred to the military transport UTX Ganymede, serving under Captain Britten before finally commandign the USCSS Nostromo.

Thomas Kane

Executive Officer

Gilbert Ward Kane, better known as Thomas Kane, was a star pupil throughout his early education, graduating with honors from Weatherly Private School before becoming top of his class at Gunning Foss Military School. Later on, Kane would attend Wellington Academy in Suffolk, England, where he obtained a Masters in engineering and a GBUK pilot's license. Before being posted as Executive Officer of the USCSS Nostromo, he boarded the UKMS Outreach, the USCSS Giler and the UKCS Powell, serving as a captain on this one.

Ellen Ripley

Warrant Officer

Ellen Ripley was born on January 7, 2092 at the Olympia colony on Luna. After gaining a Masters in engineering from the New York Aeronautics University, she served with the US Merchant Navy aboard the Zelazny, where she acted as co-pilot. By the 2120s, Ripley was living in a small town on the coast of El Salvador and serving as a warrant officer and third in command aboard the commercial freighter USCSS Nostromo.

Ash

Science Officer

Ash is a Hyperdyne Systems 120-A/2 synthetic (Android) that was created for Weyland-Yutani in the early part of the 22nd century. Little is known about his activities prior to 2122, except that he was a synthetic employee for the Corporation. He was a synthetic sleeper agent who had been placed on board the Nostromo specifically to ensure the Xenomorph on LV-426 was returned to the company, for study and use in their bio-weapons division.

Joan Lambert

Navigation Officer

Joan Marie Lambert was born on November 7, 2093 in Ontario, Canada. After growing up in Arkansas, she studied astronavigation at the Florida Aeronautics Institute, graduating with S-2 rating in FTL navigation. The Nostromo is her first posting.

Dennis Parker

Navigation Officer

Dennis Parker earned his professional qualifications at the ICC Engineering Trade School's Martian campus, graduating with a Tech 2 degree. Prior to his posting to the Nostromo, Parker served as engineer aboard the cargo vessel USCSS Nonnabo, where in 2117 he was joined by Samuel Brett. Both eventually transferred to the Nostromo together in 2120.

Joan Lambert

Navigation Officer

After failing to graduate from the Nicholson Public High School in Hawks, West Virginia, Samuel Brett studied at the ICC Engineering Trade School on Earth, where he earned a degree in engineering. Prior to his posting to the Nostromo, Brett was assigned to the USCSS Nonnabo along side Navigation officer, Dennis Parker. Being an engineer, Brett is an adept with a wide range of tools and mechanicalequipment aboard the ship.

Jones

Ship's Cat

Jones, nicknamed "Jonesy", is a ginger American Shorthair Tomcat kept aboard the USCSS Nostromo as Ripley's pet. While the cat's official purpose was to control rodents aboard the ship, it also served as a source of relaxation and entertainment for the crew on long space journeys.
